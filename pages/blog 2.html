Curren watch

matthew friesen

28 de julio de 2024

I had one before and r this is the fourth one I bought now from Curren, and have been happy with the watchers thus far. That was up until today. When my watch arrived today two hands were off of their pins one hour marker was loose and floating around also. And the winding dial on the side was broken and came right out with very little effort. So I'm not impressed with this watch at all! As I said I'd like the one other ones in the past and that's why I opted to get yet another one in a different colo,r create a collection. I hope the distributor or the manufacturer reads this and does something about it otherwise I'm sending it back for a refund
